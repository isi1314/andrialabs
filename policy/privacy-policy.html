Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, email, and payment details. We also gather technical data like IP addresses, browser type, and usage via cookies to enhance your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to deliver and improve services, communicate updates, process payments, and personalize your experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our team regularly reviews security protocols to maintain data safety.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Cookies help us improve website functionality, analyze traffic, and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may impact site performance.

5. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third parties like payment processors and analytics providers who handle data on our behalf. These partners are required to protect your information and only use it as agreed.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time using the link in our emails.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect legal or operational changes. Please review it regularly to stay informed.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
